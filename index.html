My research lies at the intersection of generative modeling, signal processing, and learning for spatial inference. More recently, I am interested in diffusion models for inverse problems. I also received an MS degree in ECE from UIUC. In a previous life, I have worked on reinforcement learning, bandits, and wireless networks, as a researcher at TCS Research. I received my B.Tech. in Electrical Engineering from IIT Tirupati in 2019, advised by Prof. K. P. Naveen.
